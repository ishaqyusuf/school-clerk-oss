# 24 — Procurement, Purchases, And Payees

**What to build:** Operators can record purchases, service payments, vendor bills, direct expenses, reimbursements, and reusable vendor/payee histories funded from accounts.

**Blocked by:** 21 — Money-Out And Needs-Funding Payables

**Status:** ready-for-agent

- [ ] Operators can record a purchase with vendor/payee, item/service description, quantity where needed, cost, funding account, term ledger, payment status, receipt/reference, and notes.
- [ ] Operators can record service payments, vendor bills, direct expenses, and reimbursements with clear labels.
- [ ] Purchases can be paid immediately or recorded as unpaid payables.
- [ ] Purchase and payment records appear in the relevant account statement.
- [ ] Vendors and non-staff payees can be reused across purchases/payments.
- [ ] Purchase cancellation, refund, and correction behavior preserves audit history.
- [ ] Tests cover immediate purchase, unpaid vendor bill, service payment, reimbursement, reusable payee, account statement effects, refund, and correction behavior.
