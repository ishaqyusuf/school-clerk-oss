# 19 — Collected-In Vs Paid-For Term Attribution

**What to build:** Payments distinguish the term where cash is collected from the academic term the payment is for, including receipts and arrears visibility.

**Blocked by:** 17 — Term Ledger Foundation; 18 — Term-Aware Accounts And Statements

**Status:** ready-for-agent

- [ ] Student payments can record both collected-in term and paid-for term.
- [ ] A payment collected in the current term for a previous term affects the current term cash/account ledger.
- [ ] The previous-term obligation is reduced or annotated through allocation without adding new cash directly to a closed term.
- [ ] Receipts show payment date, collected-in term, paid-for term, account affected, and amount paid.
- [ ] Previous-term arrears are visible from the current bursary workspace.
- [ ] Tests cover same-term payment, late payment for previous term, closed-term protection, receipt display data, and arrears updates.
